Registration

     We would like to express our appreciation and congratulation for the distinguished works you had contributed to Chinese Search Based Software Engineering 2013. Please follow the following instruction to accomplish the required procedures for conference registration.

     All attendees for CSBSE2013 are kindly requested to register. There are two types of registration: free and regular registration, the first way is no charge, and the second way need pay 800RMB(Chinese Yuan) for registration fee in the form of bank transfer, bank transfer will be available soon. The registration fee includes sightseeing, souvenir and banquet, but it doesn’t cover accommodation, transportation etc. Please fill in the form for Registrant Information by E-mail to csbse2013@163.com before June 1 for registration.

     Please feel free to let us know if you have any problem regarding the conference registration. Thank you again for your contribution to CSBSE2013. We look forward to meeting you in Dalian.
