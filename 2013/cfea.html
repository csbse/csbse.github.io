Call for Extended Abstract

     Authors are invited to electronically submit their extended abstracts, written in English, of up to 2 single spaced pages, presenting the results of original research or innovative practical applications relevant to the conference. Practical experiences with state-of-the-art search based methodologies are also acceptable when they reflect lessons of unique value to the conference attendees.
